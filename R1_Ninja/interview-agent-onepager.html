The solution What InterviewIQ does

InterviewIQ is an AI agent that joins your Microsoft Teams call as a participant. It reads the job description and candidate profile, generates a tailored question bank, conducts a seamless Test + R1 + R2 interview via voice, monitors the candidate's screen, webcam, and audio for integrity signals in real time, and delivers a structured hire / reject verdict with a full scorecard the moment the session ends — without a human interviewer in the room.

Discovery & scoping What we're building and what's in scope

JD & profile ingestion

Upload or paste any JD and résumé. AI extracts skills, seniority, domain, and gap areas to define the interview focus automatically.

Adaptive question engine

Pre-generates a question bank and selects the next question based on the candidate's last answer — not a fixed script.

Teams bot integration

Bot joins as a call participant via Microsoft Graph API. Speaks questions using Azure TTS, listens and transcribes via Azure STT.

Screen share analysis

Receives and analyses the candidate's screen share. OCR detects open apps, URLs, and blocked tools in real time.

Eye & face proctoring

Webcam feed tracked via MediaPipe. Detects gaze direction, face absence, multiple people, and lip-sync mismatches.

Audio integrity monitoring

Background voice detection, earphone heuristics, and keystroke-while-speaking signals flag coaching attempts in real time.

Real-time scoring engine

Every answer scored against a pre-built rubric — STAR adherence, technical accuracy, communication clarity, confidence signals.

Verdict & report generation

Hire / reject verdict with scorecard, transcript highlights, integrity log, radar chart, and one-click ATS export.

InterviewIQ plugs directly into Interview Ninja — your existing interview management portal

Interview Ninja already holds role templates, curated question banks, answer guidelines, and scoring rubrics built by your team. InterviewIQ reads these automatically at session start, uses them to conduct the interview, and writes results back — so nothing is re-entered manually and everything appears in Interview Ninja as if an interviewer filled it in.

Interview Ninja provides →

  • 📋Role-specific question templates per job
  • ✅Expected answers & answer guidelines per question
  • ⭐Scoring rubric — weightage per question
  • 🔒Authenticity & integrity scoring criteria
  • 👤Candidate record & session metadata
Pull at session start
Push on session end

InterviewIQ writes back →

  • 🎙Candidate answer transcript per question
  • 📊AI-generated score mapped to Ninja's rubric
  • 🚩Integrity flags against authenticity fields
  • 💬AI commentary per answer (as interviewer notes)
  • 🏆Overall verdict synced to candidate record
No duplicate data entry. Interviewers who currently fill Interview Ninja manually will find the same fields auto-populated after every AI-conducted session. The recruiter can review, add a note, or override any field — Interview Ninja remains the single source of truth for all interview records.
